Description

A pizza is a beloved Italian dish consisting of a round, flat dough base topped with a variety of ingredients, then baked to perfection. Traditionally, it features a tomato sauce layer, melted cheese (typically mozzarella), and an assortment of toppings such as pepperoni, vegetables, meats, and herbs. The crust can range from thin and crispy to thick and chewy, depending on regional styles like Neapolitan, New York-style, or deep-dish Chicago pizza. Whether classic Margherita or loaded with toppings, pizza offers endless customization and is enjoyed worldwide as a comfort food staple.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese(Mozzarella)
  • Pepperoni(Optional)
  • Vegetables(Optional)

Steps

  1. Prepare the Dough
  2. Roll Out the Dough
  3. Add Sauce & Toppings
  4. Bake the Pizza
  5. Slice & Enjoy
